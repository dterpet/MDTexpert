Who We Are

Precision. Credibility.
Results.

Meridian Expert Group is a premier litigation consulting firm connecting legal teams with exceptional subject matter experts across a broad spectrum of disciplines. Our experts have testified in federal and state courts, arbitration panels, and regulatory proceedings worldwide.

We work exclusively with vetted professionals — former executives, academics, regulators, and industry leaders — whose credentials withstand the most rigorous cross-examination. Every engagement is handled with absolute discretion and a single, unwavering standard: your client's best outcome.
